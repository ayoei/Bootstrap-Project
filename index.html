ABOUT M.O.C

National Automobile Museum - Schlumpf Collection
The world's largest automobile collection with nearly 400 historic, prestige or racing cars. This museum, housed in a 25,000 m2 former spinning mill, has been transformed into a showcase for more than 400 exceptional cars retracing automotive history.

TICKETS TO M.O.C

Secure tickets easily via the museum's website or app. Explore online discounts for budget-friendly options and consider joining the membership program for exclusive perks. Plan ahead to enjoy priority access to the captivating world of cars.
